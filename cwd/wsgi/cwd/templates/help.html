HOW TO PLAY

This demo is based on the NYT WORDLE game.

Guess the WORD in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word.

Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples

The letter F is in the word and in the correct spot.

The letter L is in the word but in the wrong spot.

The letters K, A, O, W and S are not in the word in any spot.

Speak to the Confluent team about the competition and how to win!
